ArcStash is a non-custodial savings vault on Arc Testnet. Commit to a lock period, earn the habit, and unlock your funds on your terms.

How it works
Step 1
Get Test Tokens
Claim free testnet USDC or EURC from the Circle faucet to get started.
Step 2
Connect Wallet
Connect MetaMask, Rabby, or any EVM wallet. Arc Testnet is added automatically.
Step 3
Deposit & Lock
Choose your token, enter the amount, and set a lock period. Your funds are secured onchain.
Step 4
Withdraw
Once the timer expires, withdraw your full amount. No fees, no middleman.
